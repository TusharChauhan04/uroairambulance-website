Flight Medical Escort Services

At UroAirAmbulance, we recognize that traveling for medical purposes can be exhausting - physically and emotionally - especially during periods of unwellness or recovery. Our Medical Escort Service is designed to provide the appropriate level of assistance for those with medical needs when they are ready to travel, but do not require a full air ambulance.

Our trained medical professionals provide continuous care and monitoring from departure to final destination.
